Roti with Beef Chutney



Description

A flavourful dish that is popular with local Durban families. These mince kebabs are served mainly with rotis or salad.

Ingredients for kebabs

  • Salt to taste
  • One teaspoon each of garum, dhania and jeera powder
  • Coriander, finely chopped
  • Two green chillies, finely chopped
  • One onion, finely chopped
  • 500g of lean beef mince
  • One egg for binding

Ingredients for chutney

  • 10 to 12 grated tomatoes
  • Two onions, finely chopped
  • Two sprigs of curry leaves
  • One tablespoon of curry powder
  • One teaspoon of sugar
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bind all the Ingredients and shape into kebabs
  2. Grill for about 45 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius
  3. Fry the onions in oil until golden brown
  4. Then add to onions the curry powder, tomatoes, curry leaves and salt
  5. Cook until completely dry
  6. Add sugar and kebabs
  7. Mix gently
  8. Garnish with coriander, serve with roti and salad
