Homemade Marinara Sauce


Description

A rich homemade Marinara Sauce



Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2.2kg chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 large basil sprig
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add the garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until it turns golden, approx. 3-4 minutes
  3. Add the tomatoes, italian seasoning, basil, 1 tsp salt and a few grinds of black pepper
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for 7 minutes
  5. Take off the heat and stir in the butter until melted.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
