Lasagna Crockpot Recipe



Description

A comforting Lasagna dish made using a crockpot (slow cooker)


Cooking Tips

  • Use a mix of hamburger and italian sausage for great flavour
  • Break off the corners of the lasagna sheets to fit in the slow cooker
  • Soak your noodles in water for a few minutes if you prefer a softer noodle. If you prefer al dente, follow the instructions below
  • Top with basil or parsley for extra flovour

Ingredients


  • 225g ground beef
  • 225g ground italian sausage
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 700ml marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of water, divided
  • 450g ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 500g pack of lasagna sheets (12 sheets per pack)
  • Fresh basil for serving

Instructions


  1. Spray the slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray
  2. Brown the hamburger and italian sausage in a pan over medium heat
  3. Drain grease then add italian seasoning and onions
  4. Cook until onions are soft
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce with 1/2 cups water
  6. Let sauce mixture simmer for 5 minutes
  7. While the sauce is simmering, mix together the ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese and egg
  8. Spread a cup's worth of sauce mixture onto the bottom of the crockpot
  9. Cover this layer of mixture with 3 noodle sheets (you will need to break corners off to make the sheets fit the crockpot)
  10. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture on top of the noodle sheets
  11. Repeat the above process until your ingredients are used up
  12. Add 1/2 cup water at the very end
  13. If your noodles are very thick, add an extra 2 tbsp of water
  14. Sprinkle the remaining cup of shredded mozarella cheese on top
  15. Cook on low setting for 4-5 hours or until noodles are tender
